================================================================================

================================================================================
TRIKON 3.0 – TIMELINE / SCHEDULE
================================================================================

4 April 2026:
- 8:30 AM – 11:00 AM  → Opening Ceremony and Hackathon Briefing
- 11:00 AM – 3:00 PM  → Round 1: AI Vision Blueprint
- 3:00 PM – 5:00 PM   → Evaluation: Top 25 teams selected
- 5:00 PM onwards     → Round 2: Build Sprint Alpha begins

5 April 2026:
- Until 10:00 AM      → Round 2: Build Sprint Alpha ends
- 10:00 AM – 12:30 PM → Round 3: Impact Arena (Final Presentations)
- 12:30 PM – 1:30 PM  → Results and Prize Distribution Ceremony

================================================================================
TRIKON 3.0 – ROUNDS
================================================================================

Round 1 – AI Vision Blueprint:
Teams attend the introductory session, brainstorm ideas, identify a problem statement, and create a blueprint. Must explain how AI or ML will be used.

Round 2 – Build Sprint Alpha:
Qualified teams build their prototype, implement AI models or APIs, and convert their idea into a working solution.

Round 3 – Impact Arena:
Top teams present their projects to the judging panel. Must explain tech stack, demo the prototype, and highlight real-world impact.

================================================================================
TRIKON 3.0 – TRACKS
================================================================================

Available tracks:
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- AR/VR Technologies
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Hardware Solutions
- Software Platforms
- Open Innovation

Note: AI or Machine Learning must play a core role in every project, regardless of track.

================================================================================
TRIKON 3.0 – RULES
================================================================================

- Team size: 3 to 5 members
- Members can be from different branches, years, or colleges
- Valid college ID card is mandatory
- Submit a one-page problem statement during registration
- All development must happen during the official 28-hour period
- Plagiarism is strictly prohibited
- AI tools, APIs, and pre-trained models are allowed but must be documented
- Teams must show continuous development progress
- Violations may result in disqualification

================================================================================
TRIKON 3.0 – OBJECTIVES
================================================================================

- Promote AI-driven innovation among students
- Encourage exploration of AI across industries
- Empower Women in Technology
- Build a strong developer and innovator community
- Give practical exposure to teamwork, development, and presentation
