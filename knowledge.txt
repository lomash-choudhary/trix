## ABOUT TRIKON 3.0

Trikon 3.0 is the flagship hackathon of Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology (MIET), Meerut. It is a 2-day coding and innovation hackathon where students from across the country come together to build, create, and compete. The event is organized by the students of MIET and supported by the college faculty and technical clubs.

## RULES & GUIDELINES

1. All team members must be currently enrolled students.
2. Teams must build their project from scratch during the hackathon — pre-built projects are not allowed.
3. Use of open-source libraries and APIs is permitted.
4. Projects must be original and must not plagiarize existing work.
5. Each team must submit their code on GitHub and provide a working demo.
6. Teams must present their project to the judges within the allotted time.
7. Any form of misconduct will lead to disqualification.
8. The decision of the judges is final.

---

## JUDGING CRITERIA

Projects will be evaluated on the following parameters:

- **Innovation & Creativity** (25%) – How unique and original is the idea?
- **Technical Complexity** (25%) – How technically challenging and well-built is the solution?
- **Impact & Usefulness** (20%) – Does the project solve a real problem?
- **Presentation & Demo** (20%) – How well is the project explained and demonstrated?
- **UI/UX Design** (10%) – Is the interface clean, intuitive, and user-friendly?

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

**Q: Can I participate solo?**
A: No, Trikon 3.0 requires teams of 2 to 4 members.

**Q: Can team members be from different colleges?**
A: Yes, cross-college teams are allowed and encouraged.

**Q: Is there a registration fee?**
A: Please check the official website for the latest registration fee details.

**Q: What should I bring?**
A: Bring your laptop, charger, college ID card, and any hardware components you may need for your project.

**Q: Will food and accommodation be provided?**
A: Yes, meals will be provided to all registered participants. Accommodation is available for outstation participants — please mention this during registration.

**Q: Can we use cloud services or APIs?**
A: Yes, you may use any publicly available APIs, cloud platforms, or open-source tools.

**Q: What is the last date to register?**
A: Registration deadlines will be announced on the official website. Register early to secure your spot!

**Q: Who do I contact for queries?**
A: Reach out to the Trikon 3.0 organizing team via the official email or social media handles listed on the website.
